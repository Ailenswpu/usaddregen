Recommended QA workflow

  1. Pick a state or country page that matches the form you are testing.
  2. Generate one or more synthetic records and copy the visible fields into your fixture.
  3. Keep generated street lines out of real shipping, identity, payment, banking, telecom, or government workflows.
  4. Use inbrowser.sh Mock Data Generator when you need JSON, CSV, or SQL rows around the address fields.

What this is not

This is not an address-verification service, tax engine, mail-delivery service, or identity data source. It formats internally consistent placeholder records for legal testing and demos only.

Usage disclaimer

Synthetic, realistic-format only. Not deliverable. Will fail real USPS, AVS, bank, telecom, government, or shipping verification. For QA, testing, demos, and placeholder data only. Not for fraud, account abuse, identity claims, or rule evasion.
