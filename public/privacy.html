Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how US Tax-Free State Address Generator (the “Site”), available at https://usaddregen.com/, handles information when you visit. The Site is operated as a static web tool hosted on Cloudflare. We do not require you to register an account, log in, or submit any personal information to use the address generator.

1. Information we do not collect

We do not ask for, receive, or store any of the following on our own servers:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, or any other personal identifier.
  • Account credentials — the Site has no account system.
  • Form submissions — the Site has no forms that send data to a backend.
  • Payment information — the Site is free to use and does not process payments.

2. Information stored locally in your browser

The Site uses your browser’s localStorage to remember your selected interface language so that it persists across visits. The key addressGeneratorLanguage stores a value of en, zh-CN, or zh-TW. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can clear it any time through your browser’s storage settings.

3. Server logs

Because the Site is delivered through Cloudflare’s global content delivery network, Cloudflare automatically processes basic technical request data — IP address, user agent, requested path, and timestamp — for purposes such as routing, DDoS protection, and performance analytics. This processing is governed by Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy. We do not separately aggregate or sell this data.

4. Third-party advertising — Google AdSense

We participate in the Google AdSense program. Google and its partner ad networks may use cookies, web beacons, device identifiers, and similar technologies on this Site to:

  • Serve ads based on prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Measure ad performance and detect invalid traffic.
  • Provide personalized advertising where allowed by your jurisdiction’s settings.

Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this Site and other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor’s use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use partner sites or apps, please review the Google Privacy & Terms page.

6. Children’s privacy

The Site is a developer utility intended for adult software engineers, testers, and designers. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has somehow provided personal information through the Site, please contact us so we can investigate.

7. EEA, UK, and California users

If you visit the Site from the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA. Because we do not collect personal information ourselves, the most actionable right concerns advertising cookies set by Google. Please use the Google opt-out links above to exercise consent or deletion rights regarding those cookies.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected in the page content rather than emailed, since we do not collect email addresses.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions about the Site, please reach out via X / Twitter (@ailenswpu). Because we do not collect personal data, we generally cannot look up or delete records associated with an individual user — there are no such records on our side to look up.
