Pennsylvania sales-tax background

6% statewide base sales tax. Statewide sales tax is 6%; Allegheny County and Philadelphia have additional local taxes. This page is an address-format testing utility and not tax advice. If your application needs official tax calculation, use a tax engine or the state's current tax-agency documentation.

Flat state income tax applies; local earned income taxes may apply.
