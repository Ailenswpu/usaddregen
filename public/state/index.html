US Tax-Free States

The five US states with no statewide sales tax — pick one to open its dedicated address generator. Or use the homepage tool to generate from a random tax-free state.

Delaware (DE)

ZIP 19xxx · Area code 302 · No statewide sales tax

Wilmington, Dover, Newark, Middletown, Lewes. No sales tax since statehood; Gross Receipts Tax on businesses instead. Corporate-friendly — most Fortune 500 companies are incorporated here.

Montana (MT)

ZIP 59xxx · Area code 406 · No general statewide sales tax

Billings, Missoula, Bozeman, Helena, Great Falls. No general sales tax, but a small number of resort towns (Big Sky, Whitefish, etc.) impose local-option taxes on lodging and prepared food.

New Hampshire (NH)

ZIP 03xxx · Area code 603 · No sales tax, no wage income tax

Manchester, Nashua, Concord, Portsmouth, Dover. “Live Free or Die.” A targeted Meals & Rooms tax (~8.5%) applies to restaurants and lodging but not general retail.

Oregon (OR)

ZIP 97xxx · Area codes 503 / 541 / 458 / 971 · No sales tax at any level

Portland, Salem, Eugene, Bend, Medford. Voters have rejected a sales tax nine times. The cleanest zero-tax US shipping address you can put in a test fixture.

Alaska (AK)

ZIP 99xxx · Area code 907 · No statewide sales tax (but local taxes apply)

Anchorage, Fairbanks, Juneau, Sitka, Ketchikan. No statewide sales tax and no state income tax, but boroughs and municipalities can impose their own 0–7.5% local sales tax. Anchorage is zero-tax; Juneau and Ketchikan are not.
