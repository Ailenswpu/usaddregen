Massachusetts sales-tax background

6.25% statewide base sales tax. Statewide sales and use tax is 6.25%; local sales taxes generally do not apply. This page is an address-format testing utility and not tax advice. If your application needs official tax calculation, use a tax engine or the state's current tax-agency documentation.

Flat state income tax applies, with surtax for high income.
